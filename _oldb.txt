### ⏹ Stopping (stop_all.bat)

Path: MyDJProdj/stop_all.bat. Run it **on its own** when you want to stop both the server and the bot.

It is **not** called from 
un_all.bat. It runs PowerShell and stops python.exe processes whose command line matches **manage.py … 
unserver** or **ot_main.py**. If you have other projects running with the same arguments, they may be stopped as well.

The contents of 
un_all.bat and stop_all.bat are also shown on the **NEO** page (/neo/).
